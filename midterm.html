Main Room

The Main Room, located along the north driveway adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center, is a treasure trove housing a diverse collection of books, including hardbound editions starting at a mere $1 and paperbacks available for just 50 cents each. This room serves as a hub for book donations, welcoming large contributions. Catering to book enthusiasts of all kinds, it's an ideal spot to peruse through an array of literary works while indulging in affordable deals. With a policy allowing the selection of only 12 books at a time during the initial hour on Saturdays, visitors can hold their chosen items at the cashiers' station until they're ready to make their final purchases.

Children's Room

Nestled near the Cubberley track, Pavilion, and the Greendell School, the Children's Room is a haven for young readers and parents alike. Here, a delightful array of children's books, music, videos, and toys are available at pocket-friendly prices, ranging from 50 cents to $1. The collection spans a wide range of materials, including picture books, school-age fiction and nonfiction, non-English language books, CDs, DVDs, and educational resources for parents and teachers. On Saturdays, this room opens an hour earlier than the Main Room, ensuring an early start for families and providing an opportunity for visitors to explore and select up to 12 items before purchase.

Bargain Room

Rooms K6 and K7 house the Bargain Books section, offering incredibly low-priced literary deals. Hardbound books are uniformly priced at $1, paperbacks at 50 cents, and children's books at a mere 25 cents. On Sundays, visitors can relish a half-off discount in the Bargain Room. Additionally, to save more, patrons can purchase grocery bags for $5 each and fill them with books. The Bargain Room opens alongside the Children's Room an hour earlier than the Main Room on Saturdays, inviting early birds to enjoy fantastic book deals and discounts.
